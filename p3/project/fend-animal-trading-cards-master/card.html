Lion

Today, fewer than 21,000 remain in all of Africa.

  • Speed: 81 kmph
  • Mating Season: Throughout the year
  • Litter Size: 3-4 cubs
  • Conservation Status: Vunerable of extinction

Renowned for its majesty and nicknamed "the king of the jungle," the lion possesses both beauty and strength. Lions vary in color but typically sport light yellow-brown coats. Mature male lions are unique among big cats due the thick brown or black manes that encircle their necks.
